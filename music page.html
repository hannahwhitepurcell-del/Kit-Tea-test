They have a mix of genres in their music, like hip-hop, pop and rock. They have over 25 albums that have been produced since 2013. Most of their songs are in Koreans but as they have grown in popularity, they have done some songs in English, like " Dynamite " and “swim”, their new song. They also write their own songs as well, mainly Suga and RM, but the other members have gotten a lot more involved in the past few years. Their lyrics are the reason a lot of people like them as artists; they sing about mental health, loss and self-love. They also get along with each other, and people also like them because of their personalities and how they interact with each other. They have done reality shows together like “Bon Vonage”, and it is a great way for ARMY to get to know the members more and to understand their personalities.
